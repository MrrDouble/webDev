About "Sunflower"
Sunflower is based off the upcoming movie Into The Spiderverse, and will be played in the movie's soundtrack. It was made to create a comic-book vibe for the movie, but perhaps it was made to represent Post Malone's ex as the "sunflower. The reason why I favor this song as not only does it theme around Spider-Man, but the entire mood and visuals of the song are incredible in my opinion. I stated before that the movie was themed around the comics of Spider-Man, and this song's clips and chill vocals enhance it even more. It's just a song that calms me done, feels happy, and feels free.
